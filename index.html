MinexFast what is it?

Minex Fast is a licensed cryptocurrency production facility based in the UK. since 2017, it has been producing in-house and in its own interests. In the first quarter of 2020, Europe also offered its activities to the public and carried out part of the project in the form of joint production with users. Turkey, Russia and the United Arab Emirates also started operating in 2021, and the number of participants exceeded the planned strategy. Currently, 40% of the MinexFast LTD facility is shared with users. MinexFast continues to operate in the Turkish and Asian markets under the name of MinexFast.
