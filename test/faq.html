WHAT IS BITCOIN?

Bitcoin is the most popular cryptocurrency that is generated by networks of computers and can be used in many ways to make online payments. Bitcoins are “born” thanks to miners (“miners”). Mining is the process of generating cryptocurrency using computing equipment. Miners are rewarded for mining bitcoins. The bitcoin exchange rate is not subject to banks and financial regulators. However, you can’t exactly call it stable either. The cost of this cryptocurrency (like many others) is capable of making wild jumps both up and down.
